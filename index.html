'HOW TO GET THERE

1.Ryanair flies directly from Dublin to Girona on Wednesdays and Sundays. Girona airport is only 15 minutes away from Girona City centre. There are shuttle buses from Girona airport to Girona city centre every hour (otherwise a taxi costs approximatly €30).

2.Aerlingus and Ryanair fly from Dublin to Barcelona every day. From Barcelona airport there are 2 ways to get to Girona:

There are regular trains connecting Barcelona airport and Barcelona Sants station (20 min). From Barcelona Sants station there are many trains to Girona. Check the following website for timetables Trains ; we aware that there is a fast train (AVE/AVANT) that only takes 35 minutes to Girona!

There is a direct bus from Barcelona Nord bus station (located in Barcelona city centre) to Girona. It’s longer than the train (75 minutes). Check website for details: Buses
